What is Artificial Intelligence (AI)?

English: AI is the ability of machines to learn, think, and make decisions like humans. Hindi: AI वह technology है जो मशीनों को इंसानों की तरह सीखने और सोचने की क्षमता देती है। Pronunciation: AI vah technology hai jo mashīnoṅ ko insānoṅ kī tarah sīkhne aur sochne kī kṣamatā detī hai.

Machine Learning (ML)

Machines ko data se sikhaaya jaata hai. Jaise – spam email ko automatically पहचानना.

Deep Learning

Human brain jaise neural networks use hote hain. Jaise – face recognition, voice assistant, self-driving cars.

Real Life AI Examples

YouTube recommendation, Google Maps, Chatbots, Online shopping suggestion – sab mein AI ka use hota hai.

Why Should You Learn AI?

High Demand Career

AI engineers, data scientists, ML developers – sab ki demand bahut high hai aur salary भी strong hoti hai.

Future Technology

आने वाले समय में हर field – education, health, business – sab mein AI ka bahut बड़ा role hoga.

Problem Solving Skill

AI सीखने se tumhara logical thinking, maths aur coding skill भी improve hota hai.
